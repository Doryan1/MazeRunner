The Maze Runner

The one who maze

As a ghost of the night, Bob is seen as one of the best puzzle solvers in the world. His love for mazes has brought him here. Are you able to help Bob complete the numerous amounts of mazes?

How To Play

This game is played with the arrow keys of the keyboard. The objective is to move left, right, up,and down to get out of the maze. The faster you get to the end the cooler you are
